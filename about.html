About Spamsparx

Learn about the mission behind Spamsparx and the passion driving its creation.

Passion for Cyber security

In an increasingly digital world, the safety of our online interactions is more important than ever. I’m Julisha Rose Joby, a Creative Computing graduate with a deep passion for cybersecurity. Driven by a desire to protect individuals and organizations from digital threats, I’ve dedicated myself to making the internet a safer space for everyone.

My interest in cybersecurity was sparked by witnessing the real-world consequences of phishing attacks and other online threats. This fueled my determination to create accessible tools and resources that empower people to better understand and protect themselves in the digital landscape.

Spamsparx was born from this mission – a platform committed to demystifying cybersecurity and providing practical, easy-to-use solutions for everyday users. Whether you’re tech-savvy or just getting started, my goal is to make online safety understandable, approachable, and achievable for all.

Our Vision & Mission

Empowerment

To empower users with the knowledge and tools needed to identify and protect themselves against phishing threats effectively.

Awareness

To raise awareness about the evolving nature of phishing attacks and promote a culture of cybersecurity vigilance.

Accessibility

To provide simple, free, and accessible resources that make cybersecurity awareness straightforward for everyone.
